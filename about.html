About Us

Crafting Quality Prints
Since 1991

From a garage workshop to a trusted screen printing company — our story is driven by quality, creativity, and commitment.

35+ Years of Excellence

Bright Screen started as a small passion project in a garage workshop and has grown into one of the region's most trusted screen printing companies. Our journey has been driven by a relentless commitment to quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction.

We invest in state-of-the-art equipment and continuous training to ensure every print meets the highest standards. From a single-color logo to complex multi-color artwork, we treat every project with the same level of care and precision.
