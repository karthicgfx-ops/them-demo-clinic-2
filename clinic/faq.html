FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

Appointments & Booking

You can book online through our website by clicking any "Book Appointment" button — this opens a calendar where you can pick a date and time that works for you. You can also call us directly at +91 99999 88888 or visit the clinic in person. Walk-ins are welcome, though appointments help reduce wait times.
Yes. You can reschedule or cancel your appointment directly through the booking confirmation link sent to your email or phone. We kindly ask for at least 24 hours notice so we can offer the slot to another patient.
Please bring a valid government-issued photo ID, your insurance card (if applicable), a list of current medications, and any previous medical records or test reports. Arriving 15 minutes early helps us complete the registration smoothly.
Yes. We offer video consultations for follow-up visits and select primary care concerns. Telemedicine appointments can be booked through the same online booking system — just select the "Video Visit" option when choosing your appointment type.

Medical Services

We have six core departments: Cardiology, Neurology, Orthopedics, Pediatrics, Dermatology, and General Medicine. Each department is led by experienced, board-certified specialists. Visit our Departments page for detailed information about each service.
Absolutely. We offer dedicated second opinion consultations where our specialists review your diagnosis and treatment plan. This service ensures you have complete confidence in your medical decisions. Just mention "second opinion" when booking.
Our clinic has a fully equipped diagnostic wing with digital X-ray, ultrasound, echocardiography, ECG, stress test, blood lab, and advanced imaging referral services. Most tests are completed during your visit with results available the same day.
Yes. We have a dedicated emergency care unit staffed 24/7 with trained emergency physicians and nurses. For life-threatening emergencies, please call +91 99999 88888 or visit our emergency department directly.

Insurance & Billing

We accept a wide range of major insurance networks including ICICI Lombard, Star Health, Apollo Munich, HDFC Ergo, Niva Bupa, and many others. We also work with corporate insurance plans and third-party administrators. Please contact our billing department to verify your specific plan coverage before your visit.
We accept cash, all major credit and debit cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, and health insurance cards. We also offer easy EMI options for surgical procedures through partner financial institutions.
No cancellation fee if you cancel at least 24 hours before your appointment. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a nominal fee of ₹250 to ensure we can continue offering quality care to all patients.

Clinic & Visiting

We are open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We remain closed on Sundays. The emergency department operates 24/7, including Sundays and public holidays.
Yes. We encourage patients to bring a family member or friend for support. For pediatric and geriatric patients, at least one attendant is recommended. Waiting areas are spacious and comfortable for companions.
Yes. We have complimentary parking available for both two-wheelers and four-wheelers. Valet parking is available during peak hours for patient convenience. The clinic is also well-connected by public transport.
You can request copies of your medical records at the reception desk. Digital copies are provided free of charge. Physical copies may involve a nominal processing fee. We maintain strict confidentiality in accordance with privacy regulations.

Doctors & Specialists

All our doctors are board-certified specialists with extensive training from leading medical institutions. Each physician brings 10-20 years of clinical experience. Visit our Doctors page to read individual profiles and credentials.
Yes. When booking online, you can select your preferred doctor from the available slots. If you need help choosing, our front desk team will gladly help match you with the right specialist based on your symptoms and preferences.
Yes. We maintain continuity of care — your follow-up visits will be scheduled with the same doctor whenever possible. This ensures your treatment history is well understood and your care plan remains consistent.
