How It Works

Using Your Insurance

  • 1
    Verify Your Coverage
    Call us or bring your insurance card to your first visit. Our billing team will verify your coverage and explain any co-pays or deductibles upfront.
  • 2
    Receive Treatment
    Get the care you need. We'll handle the insurance paperwork for cashless claims where applicable, so you can focus on your health.
  • 3
    Settlement & Follow-Up
    We file the claim directly with your insurer. Any balance or uncovered portion will be clearly communicated before any payment is due.

Not Sure About Your Plan?

Our dedicated billing team can help verify your insurance coverage before your visit. Just give us a call or send us a message with your policy details.
