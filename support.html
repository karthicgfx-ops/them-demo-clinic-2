How Can We Help?

Find answers to common questions or reach out to our support team for assistance.

We accept AI, EPS, PDF, PSD, and high-resolution PNG/JPG files (300 DPI minimum). Vector formats are preferred for the best results, as they scale without losing quality.

Our minimum order is 24 pieces for standard screen printing. For smaller quantities, we offer direct-to-garment (DTG) printing with no minimum order.

Standard turnaround is 5-7 business days after design approval. Rush orders are available for an additional fee with 2-3 day turnaround.

Yes! Our in-house design team can create custom artwork from scratch or refine your existing designs. Design fees vary based on complexity.

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If there's any defect in our printing, we'll reprint your order at no extra cost. Custom orders are non-refundable.
